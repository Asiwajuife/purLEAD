FAQ

Questions we get a lot.

How fast will I see results?+
Most clients receive their first booked call within 2–4 weeks of launch. Month one typically produces 50–70% of full capacity as the system warms and optimizes.
Do I need to write any of the emails myself?+
No. Our team writes all copy and our AI personalizes every message. We’ll have an onboarding call to learn your voice and positioning — then we handle everything else.
Will this work in my industry?+
If your clients are businesses (B2B) and your average deal value is $5K+, outbound email works. We’ve run campaigns in SaaS, consulting, fintech, professional services, media, and more.
How is this different from a regular email agency?+
Traditional agencies use templates. We use AI to research each prospect and write genuinely personalized messages — referencing their company, role, recent news, and specific pain points. The difference in reply rates is dramatic. We also manage replies and handle booking, not just sending.
What if I don’t hit the meeting targets?+
We take accountability seriously. If we consistently fall short, we’ll discuss plan adjustments, strategy pivots, or additional support at no extra cost. We succeed when you succeed.
Is there a long-term contract?+
We ask for a 3-month initial commitment — outbound takes time to fully optimize. After that, it’s month-to-month. Most clients stay because they see results, not because they’re locked in.
